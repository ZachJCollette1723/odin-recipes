Sneaky Elixir

Description

What's worse than doing something wrong and being caught? Cut that crap out with this recipe. After slurping this down, you'll be less detectable somehow.

Ingredients

  • Sunset Firefly
  • Monster Parts

Steps

  1. Find a firely at sunset.
  2. Get, like, a part off a monster.
  3. Throw it in a skillet over an open flame for 5 seconds.
  4. Voila! Now you're ready to be real sneaky.
