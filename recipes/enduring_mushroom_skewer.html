Enduring Mushroom Skewer

Description

A good thief needs good stamina. After darting away from the scene of a crime until exhaustion, hastily eat this entire skewer and you'll be ready to double that distance!

Ingredients

  • Endura Shroom
  • That's it!

Steps

  1. Find an endura shroom or substitution
  2. Throw it in a cauldron and stir for 5 seconds
  3. Voila! It should automatically skew itself and now you can run twice as long! Repeat for infinite endurance.
